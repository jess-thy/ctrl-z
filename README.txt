# Habit Tracker - CTRL-Z

A simple web app designed to help you break bad habits by tracking your daily progress in maintaining a streak. Set a habit to break, define how many days you want to maintain the streak, and click on circles each day to track your progress.

## Features:
- **Habit Tracking**: Input your habit and set a goal streak.
- **Progress Circles**: View circles representing the days of your streak. Click each circle to mark your progress.
- **Streak Management**: Check how many days you've completed, and reset your streak when necessary.
- **Local Storage**: Your progress is saved in the browser, so it persists across sessions.

## Technologies Used:
- **HTML**: For structuring the web app.
- **CSS**: For styling the app and creating responsive layouts.
- **JavaScript**: For functionality, including local storage management and dynamic updates.

## How to Use:
1. **Start a Streak**: Enter your habit and the number of days you want to maintain your streak.
2. **Track Progress**: Click on each circle to mark your daily progress.
3. **Reset Streak**: When you're done, or if you want to start fresh, click the "Start New Streak" button.
